The project !

This project focuses on innovation under the theme "Web3 for Good", aiming to support individuals with disabilities. In collaboration with Intersport, it introduces a system that facilitates donations to associations dedicated to helping people with disabilities. By combining advanced technology and social responsibility, the initiative seeks to create meaningful and lasting impact.
